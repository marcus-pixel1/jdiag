Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

For general inquiries, we typically respond within 24 business hours. For urgent technical support, our team is available 24/7 and aims to respond within 4 hours.

I need immediate technical assistance. What should I do?

For immediate technical support, please call our 24/7 support line at +1 (800) JDIAG-TECH or use the live chat feature on our website. For the fastest resolution, please have your device serial number ready.

How do I become a JDIAG dealer?

To become an authorized JDIAG dealer, select "Become a Dealer" from the inquiry type in our contact form, or visit our Dealer Program page for detailed information and requirements.

Where can I find my device's serial number?

The serial number can be found on the back of your device, on the original packaging, or by navigating to Settings > About on your JDIAG diagnostic tool.
