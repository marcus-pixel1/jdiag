Professional Motorcycle Diagnostics Reimagined

The M400 PRO is the most comprehensive intelligent motorcycle diagnostic tool on the market, engineered for professional mechanics and passionate enthusiasts who demand precision, versatility, and reliability. With industry-leading brand coverage, advanced ECU programming capabilities, and a rugged design built for daily workshop use, the M400 PRO represents the pinnacle of motorcycle diagnostic technology.

Advanced ECU Programming

Unlike other diagnostic tools, the M400 PRO offers full ECU programming capabilities, allowing you to:

  • Fine-tune fuel maps for improved performance
  • Adjust ignition timing for better power delivery
  • Optimize throttle response for smoother operation
  • Install manufacturer updates and patches

Unmatched Brand Coverage

Why Choose the M400 PRO?

The M400 PRO offers several critical advantages over other models in the JDIAG lineup:

Frequently Asked Questions

How often do I need to update the software?

We recommend checking for updates monthly. Major updates with new vehicle models and features are typically released quarterly. Your M400 PRO includes free updates for the first year.

Is training available for this device?

Yes, we offer comprehensive online training resources with your purchase, including video tutorials and documentation. Premium in-person training is also available for an additional fee.

What's the difference between the M400 PRO and M400 Online?

The M400 PRO includes advanced ECU programming capabilities, supports more languages, covers more brands, and works offline. The M400 Online requires constant internet connection and lacks ECU programming features.

How much does it cost to renew the software subscription?

After the included first year, annual software subscription renewal costs $299. Multi-year discounts are available, with 2-year plans at $499 (save $99) and 3-year plans at $699 (save $198).

Can I use this on multiple motorcycles?

Yes, the M400 PRO is designed for multi-brand diagnostics with no limits on the number of vehicles. With 79 brands supported, it works with virtually any motorcycle you'll encounter.

What if I need technical support?

Technical support is included with your purchase. Our support team is available via phone, email, and live chat. For complex issues, we also offer remote diagnostic assistance.
