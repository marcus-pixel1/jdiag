Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the M400 PRO

How often does the M400 PRO receive software updates?

The M400 PRO receives quarterly major updates with new features and expanded vehicle coverage. Minor updates and bug fixes are released as needed, typically monthly. All updates are available via WiFi and are included free for the first year.

What's the difference between the M400 Online and the M400 PRO?

The M400 PRO is our premium offline model that includes advanced ECU programming features, multi-language support, and broader brand coverage. Unlike the Online version which requires constant internet connection, the PRO model works independently without an internet connection after initial setup, making it ideal for workshops in any location.

Can I use the M400 PRO for multiple motorcycles and brands?

Yes, the M400 PRO is designed for multi-brand diagnostic work. With support for 79 motorcycle brands, you can use a single tool for virtually any motorcycle that comes into your shop. The package includes 16 different adapters to ensure compatibility with different diagnostic ports across manufacturers.

Is training provided with purchase?

Yes, each M400 PRO purchase includes access to our online training portal with comprehensive video tutorials and documentation. We also offer optional live training sessions and can arrange personalized training for your team. The intuitive interface design means most technicians can be productive immediately with minimal training.

What happens after the first year of free updates?

After the first year, you can purchase an annual update subscription that provides all software updates, new vehicle coverage, and enhanced features. The subscription costs $299/year, which is significantly less than purchasing individual updates. Even without updates, your M400 PRO will continue to function with all its current capabilities.
