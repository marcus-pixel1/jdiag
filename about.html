Who We Are

JDIAG is a global leader in motorcycle diagnostic technology, dedicated to empowering technicians and riders with intelligent solutions that make motorcycle maintenance more accurate, efficient, and accessible.

Founded in 2010 by a team of motorcycle enthusiasts and electronics engineers, we've grown from a small startup focused on basic diagnostic tools to an international company serving more than 30,000 professional technicians across 36 countries.

Our headquarters in Silicon Valley, California, houses our R&D center, while our manufacturing facilities in Shenzhen, China, produce our award-winning diagnostic equipment. With regional offices in Europe and Asia, we provide local support to our global network of users and distributors.

Our Journey

From garage startup to global leader

2010: The Beginning

JDIAG was founded by Michael Chen, a motorcycle enthusiast and electrical engineer who saw the need for better diagnostic tools in his local repair shop. With a small team of 5 people working from a garage in Silicon Valley, the first prototype of the M100 was developed.

2012: First Major Success

After two years of development and testing, the M100 was officially launched and quickly gained popularity among local repair shops. By the end of the year, JDIAG had expanded to serve dealers across California and secured its first patent for EFI diagnostic technology.

2014: International Expansion

With growing demand, JDIAG opened its first manufacturing facility in Shenzhen, China, and expanded sales to Europe and Asia. The company introduced multilingual support and adapted its products to work with European motorcycle brands.

2016: Product Line Expansion

JDIAG introduced the M200 and M300 series, expanding its product range to serve different market segments. The company also established regional offices in Germany and Thailand to better support its growing international dealer network.

2018: Technology Breakthrough

A significant breakthrough in injector waveform analysis technology led to the development of the M400 prototype. JDIAG also launched its cloud platform, allowing diagnostic data to be shared and analyzed across devices.

2020: 10th Anniversary

JDIAG celebrated its 10th anniversary by launching the M400 series and reaching the milestone of serving 20,000 professional technicians worldwide. The company also received the "Innovation Excellence Award" from the International Motorcycle Repair Association.

2022: Global Leadership

JDIAG expanded its coverage to support over 100 motorcycle brands worldwide and reached 30,000 professional users. The company also established new partnerships with major motorcycle manufacturers for direct integration of diagnostic protocols.

2023: Looking Forward

Today, JDIAG continues to innovate with new AI-powered diagnostic features, enhanced cloud connectivity, and expanded training programs for technicians. The company remains committed to its mission of revolutionizing motorcycle maintenance through intelligent diagnostic solutions.
